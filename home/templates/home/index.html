Erysimum's Apothecary

Hello there! Welcome to Erysimum's Apothecary, a long time passion project of mine that has come from a half-decade of being a primarily self-taught forager and a 4 year long struggle with chronic pain.

I hope I can provide the tools for others to find relief from their chronic conditions, and I'm always open to talking about what has worked for me. Please remember that I'm not a medical professional, and that any advice and foraging notes I share are things that I find useful for myself, and your experience with these plant medicines may be completely different from mine. All of that being said, experiment and try what works for you under the care of a professional, and always double check that any herbal remedies you use do not interact with any medications you take.

I wish you the best of luck on your pain relief journey, or whatever journey has brought you here, and please don't hesitate to reach out to me through our discord server or email!
